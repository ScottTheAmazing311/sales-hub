Relationships determine how closely entities are connected to one another
Think of a 3D map where entities are cubes — closer cubes = stronger relationships

Part II

Strategic Assets: Definition & Purpose

Content with a specific purpose to achieve entity definition, entity placement, or conversion. Not keyword-driven. Not volume-based. Goal-driven and prescriptive.
04

Three Functions of Strategic Assets

01

Entity Definition

Establishing who the firm is. Making the entity unmistakable, unambiguous, and well-defined in the LLM knowledge map.

02

Entity Placement

Positioning the firm favorably in the LLM relationship map. Moving the entity cube closer to relevant practice areas and locations.

03

Conversion

Helping prospects convert once they reach the website. May not directly improve AI visibility, but still a critical business function.

FRAMEWORK PRINCIPLE

This is not one-size-fits-all. Every asset is tailored to client objectives. The shift is from keyword-driven, volume-based content to goal-driven, prescriptive content.

03
Part III

Critical Strategic Assets

The high-impact assets that directly influence entity definition, placement, and conversion. These are non-negotiable.
05

Homepage & About Page

Critical · Definition + Conversion

Homepage

The single most-visited page. The primary landing point after branded searches. Must clearly communicate identity to both robots and humans.

  • Expressly state who you are, what you do, and where you do it
  • Directly say you are a lawyer — don't imply it
  • State headquarters and city locations — don't rely on map listings alone
  • Include entities/nouns you want associated with the firm
Critical · Definition

About Page

The single most important page for defining entity on the website. Historically underutilized, now essential.

  • Requires dedicated planning as its own strategic asset
  • Must expressly state: who you are, what you do, where you do it
  • Never rely on implied information
  • Think about every entity/noun you want associated with the firm
06

Attorney Bios & Press Releases

Definition

Attorney Bio Pages

Attorneys are distinct entities from the law firm. They should be closely related in the LLM map but treated as separate entities.

  • Each attorney is their own entity with their own entity definition
  • Bio pages should strengthen the relationship between attorney and firm entities
  • Content must be specific to the individual, not the firm
Placement · GEO/AIO

Press Releases

More important today than at any point in internet history. Distributed to 200+ websites immediately.

  • Creates brand conversation across the web instantly
  • Functions as "backlinks on steroids" for GEO/AIO
  • Should be a standard ongoing asset for every client
  • Massive entity placement signal at scale
07

Hire Us Content & Service Pages

Critical · Conversion + Placement

"Hire Us" Style Content

Focus on differentiation, not generic service descriptions. "Why you are the best choice" — not "here's what we offer."

  • AMs lead conversations to uncover non-commodity differentiators
  • Examples: deep community ties, unique philosophy, long-term involvement
  • Must be pushed hard during and after onboarding
  • Differentiation is non-negotiable for AI visibility
Definition

Service Pages

Traditional pages explaining what the firm does. Still valuable as foundational entity definition content.

  • Remain important as foundational content
  • Should pair with hire-us content for maximum impact
  • Define practice areas as related entities
  • Best when combined with differentiation messaging
08

Testimonials & Trust Signals

Conversion + Placement

Testimonial & Review Pages

Dedicated review pages — not just widgets pulling Google reviews. Details matter more than settlement amounts alone.

  • Use the Before / During / After narrative format
  • Creates non-commodity, non-repeatable content
  • Addresses prospect psychology: emotionally distressed, ignorant about legal process, distrustful
  • Detailed stories outperform dollar figures
Placement + Conversion

Trust Signals & UGC

Third-party mentions carry higher inherent trust than first-party claims. Media perception of lawyers as "necessary evil" requires active trust-building.

  • Google and Facebook reviews are valuable baseline
  • Third-party mentions carry higher inherent trust value
  • Client interviews yield non-commodity content
  • Source-based quotes increase AI citation likelihood

Part IV

Supporting Strategic Assets

Assets that reinforce entity definition and placement. Important, but secondary to the critical assets above.
09

Wikidata, Infographics & Media

Day-One Task

Wikidata Entries

  • Day-one task for every new client
  • Must be factual and documentation-focused, not marketing
  • Include: legal name variations, locations, establishment year, DBA names
  • Agency handles resubmission until approval
  • Resolves Google citation confusion with multiple legal names
Engagement

Infographics

  • Underutilized visual asset with high engagement potential
  • Average user spends 42 seconds on a website and reads ~80 words
  • Visual content dramatically outperforms dense text
  • Easier to digest and share
Future State

Video & Audio

  • Video will become increasingly important as AI video understanding improves
  • People more likely to watch and listen than read
  • Audio playback widgets will become standard offering
  • Improves accessibility and addresses learning preferences

Part VI

Perfect Homepage & About Page Specs

The core requirements every homepage and about page must meet. No implied information. No assumptions. Explicit, clear, entity-driven.
11

Homepage & About Page Requirements

Requirement Details
State identity explicitly "Here's who I am, what I do, and where I do it" — directly, not by implication
Declare profession Directly say you are a lawyer. Don't assume visitors or AI will infer it from context.
State locations Directly state headquarters and all city locations. Don't rely on embedded maps or directory listings alone.
Entity association Think about the entities/nouns you want associated with the firm and explicitly include them on the page.
Avoid implied info If it's important, say it. Every assumption is a missed signal to both humans and AI.

Low-Impact Assets (Deprioritize)

Blog Content

Carries minimal weight in AI search. Should be deprioritized in favor of higher-impact strategic assets. Still provides some traditional SEO value but is no longer a strategic focus.

Glossary Definitions

Minimal impact on AI visibility. Historically emphasized in SEO but no longer a strategic priority. Time and resources are better spent on entity-defining assets.

RESOURCE ALLOCATION

Every hour spent on blog content or glossary definitions is an hour not spent on homepage optimization, about page refinement, press releases, or hire-us differentiation. Prioritize accordingly.
