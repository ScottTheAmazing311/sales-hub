Phase 1: Identify

How We Define Ourselves

The role exists to elevate the prospect experience and position Rankings as the highest-level growth partner in the legal industry.
GOALS

What We Are Trying to Do

GOAL 01

Create a Differentiated Outbound Experience

Move away from generic legal marketing outreach and create interactions that feel informed, strategic, and valuable from the first touch.

GOAL 02

Improve Pipeline Quality

Focus on qualified opportunities, stronger show rates, better handoffs, and higher-converting conversations instead of pure activity volume.

GOAL 03

Build Trust Through Insight

Lead with diagnosis, observations, competitive intelligence, and tailored value instead of scripts and templates.

GOAL 04

Develop High-Level Talent Internally

Build future AEs, strategists, enablement leaders, and operators through skill development and industry expertise.

GOAL 05

Strengthen the Rankings Brand

Ensure every touchpoint reflects the standard, professionalism, and authority Rankings wants associated with its name.

MINDSET

Behavioral Reframes

We do not "smile and dial." We research and engage.
We do not chase calendar invites. We create momentum.
We do not send generic follow-ups. We continue the conversation with value.
We do not own one touchpoint. We own the experience.
